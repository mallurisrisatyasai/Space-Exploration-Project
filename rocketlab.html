Rocket Lab

History & Overview

Rocket Lab is a private American aerospace manufacturer and small satellite launch service provider founded in 2006. The company focuses on providing cost-effective and reliable launch services for small payloads into space. Its signature vehicle, the Electron rocket, is designed to deliver small payloads to orbit with low cost and high frequency.

Famous Rockets

Rocket Lab's main rocket is the Electron rocket, which is designed for launching small satellites into low Earth orbit (LEO). The company has also introduced the Neutron rocket, designed to serve the medium-lift market and provide rapid, low-cost access to space for larger payloads.

Satellites

Rocket Lab specializes in launching small satellites, with a focus on low-cost, frequent launches for small and medium-sized payloads. The company has launched hundreds of small satellites for commercial, scientific, and government customers.

Involvement with ISS

Rocket Lab is involved in launching small payloads for research and scientific experiments that are part of various space missions, including those that contribute to the International Space Station (ISS). The company's launch services have been used to deploy satellites that provide data for Earth observation and space science.

Major Missions

Rocket Lab has launched numerous missions to deploy small satellites for various commercial and governmental organizations. Some of their key missions include the deployment of satellites for NASA and other global organizations, as well as launching a mission to Mars in collaboration with NASA.

Astronauts

Rocket Lab does not currently have astronauts or a human spaceflight program. The company's focus remains on launching small satellites to low Earth orbit and providing access to space for payloads.

International Collaborations

Rocket Lab has collaborated with several space agencies, including NASA, to provide launch services for small satellite missions. The company has worked with international clients and government agencies to support scientific missions, Earth observation, and communications projects.

Internships & Student Opportunities

Rocket Lab offers internships and student opportunities to work on cutting-edge aerospace projects. These programs give students hands-on experience in the aerospace industry, working on real-world problems in satellite launch services and rocket technology.
