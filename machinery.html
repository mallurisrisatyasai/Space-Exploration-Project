Rockets

Overview

Rockets are vehicles designed to launch payloads into space using controlled explosions and high-speed thrust. They are crucial for carrying satellites, probes, and humans beyond Earth.

Satellites

Overview

Satellites are man-made objects placed into orbit to perform tasks such as communication, weather forecasting, Earth observation, and scientific research.

Rovers

Overview

Rovers are robotic vehicles that explore the surface of other planets and moons. They carry scientific instruments to study terrain, climate, and geological activity.

Space Stations

Overview

Space stations are large, habitable satellites orbiting Earth that support long-duration missions for astronauts. They serve as research laboratories for studying microgravity, biology, space medicine, and new technologies essential for future deep space exploration.

Space Suits

Overview

Space suits are complex, life-supporting garments that protect astronauts from the harsh conditions of space. They provide oxygen, regulate temperature, shield from radiation, and enable mobility during spacewalks and surface missions.
